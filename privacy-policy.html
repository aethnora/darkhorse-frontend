Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2025

Website: https://darkhorsetrader.com

DarkHorse Trader ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform or visit our website.

By using DarkHorse Trader, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. What We Collect

We only collect data necessary to provide and improve the services we offer — namely, automated trading via your Alpaca account.

We do NOT collect:

  • Your Alpaca login credentials
  • Your personal financial information such as bank account or social security numbers

We DO collect:

  • OAuth access tokens and permissions (read-only or trade access you authorize)
  • Alpaca account data such as account ID, buying power, portfolio positions, and trade history
  • Email address and basic user profile data (if you choose to provide it)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Execute trades on your behalf based on your chosen strategy
  • Display your trading activity and performance within our dashboard
  • Improve the reliability and functionality of our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., audit logs, fraud prevention)

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or unsolicited marketing. We do not sell your data.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

We take data security seriously. All user data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored on secure servers behind firewalls
  • Never stored longer than necessary for the purpose of service delivery

Access to sensitive data is strictly limited to essential personnel under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Sharing of Information

We only share data when necessary and with trusted parties:

  • With Alpaca: To place trades on your behalf
  • With service providers: (e.g., secure cloud hosting, analytics) who process data on our behalf under contractual confidentiality
  • If required by law: To comply with a subpoena, legal process, or governmental request
  • In case of merger or acquisition: We may transfer data if DarkHorse Trader is acquired or merged, in which case users will be notified in advance

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third party for any reason.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request we erase your data (may limit platform access)
  • Objection: Limit how we process your data in certain cases
  • Revoke Access: You can disconnect Alpaca access at any time from your Alpaca dashboard

To exercise any of these rights, email us at aethnora@gmail.com

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies and basic analytics tools to:

  • Understand user behavior (e.g., page visits, performance bottlenecks)
  • Improve our platform's usability

You can opt out of non-essential cookies via your browser settings. No third-party advertising cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

DarkHorse Trader is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that a user under 18 has submitted personal information, we will promptly delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

We operate from the United States. If you access our services from outside the U.S., you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different privacy protections than your jurisdiction.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. If the changes are material, we will notify users via email or in-app message.
